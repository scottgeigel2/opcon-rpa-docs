# Introduction to VisualCron RPA

## What is it?

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) continues automation through the **last mile** — on a desktop, on the web, or on a hybrid of both. Where workflows, processes, or tasks pause for human intervention, RPA addresses manual gaps, enables end-to-end automation, and increases the efficiency of individual employees and the entire business.

## Getting started

**Robotic Process Automation** refers to the ability to record a sequence of human interactions (keystrokes and mouse movements and selections) with a program and play them back on demand.

**VisualCron RPA** is designed for banks, credit unions, and insurance companies for use in tandem with OpCon, extending its functionality and usefulness.

## FAQs

**What does VisualCron RPA automate that OpCon alone cannot?**
VisualCron RPA automates interactions with applications that lack APIs or scheduled-job interfaces — typically desktop or web applications that require keyboard and mouse input.

**Who is VisualCron RPA designed for?**
VisualCron RPA is designed for banks, credit unions, and insurance companies that already use OpCon for workload automation and want to extend that automation into manual, UI-driven processes.

## Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| RPA | Robotic Process Automation. Software that records and plays back human interactions (keystrokes, mouse actions) with applications that lack programmatic interfaces. |
| Last mile | The final manual steps in an automated process — typically UI interactions that an API-based scheduler cannot perform. |
